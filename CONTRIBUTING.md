## Before Opening Issues

- **Do not report security vulnerabilities publicly** (e.g., in issues or discussions), please refer to our [security policy](https://github.com/goulvenclech/astrolingo/blob/main/SECURITY.md).
- **Do not create issues for questions about using AstroLingo.** Instead, ask your question in our [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/goulvenclech/astrolingo/discussions/categories/q-a) or on [our Discord](https://maudit.org/chat).
- **Do not create issues for ideas or suggestions.** Instead, share your thoughts in our [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/goulvenclech/astrolingo/discussions/categories/ideas) or on [our Discord](https://maudit.org/chat).
- **Check for duplicates.** Look through existing issues and discussions to see if your topic has already been addressed.
- Please include a reproducible example to help us understand your issue.
- In general, provide as much detail as possible. No worries if it's not perfect, we'll figure it out together.

## Before submitting Pull Requests (PRs)

- **Check for duplicates.** Look through existing PRs to see if your changes have already been submitted.
- PRs don't need to be perfect. Submit your best effort, and we will gladly assist in polishing the work.
